Lima Bean

Bean is a rescue

Lima Bean was found on the streets in the South Side of Chicago on Halloween. He was drastically underweight and skittish.

Bean is a friend

Now happy and healthy, Lima Bean is a true companion. He is embraced with open arms by everyone he meets. His large eyes and haggard smile will warm the heart of any suitor.

Bean is an (aspiring) model

Always game for a quick photoshoot, this dog wear clothes surprisingly well. To see his current modeling porfolio refer to the Images page.
